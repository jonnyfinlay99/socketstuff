Pairing

Sensit-Connect

In order to pair your SENS-101 device to your home wifi, please plug your SENS-101 dongle into a power supply, and wait until the light is flashing green, then change the wifi on your mobile device to the access point "SENS-101".

After you have done the following steps, please enter in your home wifi name as you would find on the bottom of your hub into the first field and enter your wifi password in the second field.
